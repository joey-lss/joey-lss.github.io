The Phone

Generation 1

12-Month, $20M Go-to-Market Plan

Months 1-3: Foundation & Design

  • Finalize core leadership team of 20 professionals.
  • Establish key hardware partnerships and U.S. supply chain.
  • Lock industrial design, CMF, and core UX.
  • Begin development of D14's customized Android OS layer.

Months 4-6: Prototyping & Engineering

  • First hardware prototype assembly and testing (EVT).
  • Core software integration with hardware components.
  • Finalize MVNO agreements and build backend infrastructure.

Months 7-9: Refinement & Pre-Production

  • Build and test production-ready prototypes (DVT).
  • Begin FCC and carrier certification processes.
  • Initiate small-scale U.S. manufacturing runs (PVT).

Months 10-12: Production & Launch

  • Scale to full manufacturing production.
  • Execute pre-launch marketing campaign and open pre-orders.
  • Official launch of The Phone and the D14 MVNO service.
